Rev.Engineer Shem M.K Wanjila

A Father to many, a family man Teacher, and a Mentor.

Mr. Wanjila(In a white dustcoat) Doing what he loves most, With his students, probably after a refrigiration and air conditioning lecture
  • 1949 -Born in Bunuku village, Malava County, Kakamega District.
  • 1956 -Joined Magale PAG primary school, Std 1.
  • 1961 -Joined Shambarere intermidiate school.
  • 1964 -Joined Sigalaga Technical
  • 1966 -left Sigalagala Technical
  • 1967 -Joined Kabate Technical to do extra mural course in Engineering.
  • 1969 -Joined Kenya polytechnic, now Technical university to do Mechanical and Electrical Enginnering.
  • 1969 -Joined Hall Thermotank Equitorial limited, as an apprentice in refrigeration, ventilation and air conditioning.
  • 1972 -Joined Kenya polytechnic, now Technical university for higher diploma in Mechanical Enginnering.
  • 1975 -Started Working at East African Post and Telecommunication as an assistant Engineerer
  • 1975 -Married His First wife, Lornah Silako.
  • 1976 -Graduated from Technical University of Kenya
  • 1984 -Married His Second wife, Agnes Kiberenge
  • July 2002 -Decided to get saved and redicated his Life to server The Lord Jesus christ.
  • March 2004 -Retired from Telkom kenya
  • May 2004 -Joined KIBHIT(Kenya Institute of Building and Highway Technologies), as a lecturer
  • 2005- Joined Railway Training Institute as a lecturer
  • 2011 -Joined Pentecoastal Bible College Nyangori and graduated as a pastor
  • 2012- Joined PIEA School of Petroleum Studies as a lecturer
  • 2015- Ordained as a Revrend Pentecoastal Assemblies of Kenya (PAG) K
